ABOUT COMPASSION

Compassion is a leading child development charity. Working in partnership with local churches in developing countries, the charity links a child with a sponsor.
This sponsor enables Compassion to provide exceptional care for the sponsored child through every stage of their childhood.
Such as providing an opportunity to attend or stay in school, medical care, food, water, mentoring and a safe environment provided by their local church.
